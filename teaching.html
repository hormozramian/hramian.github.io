    Syallabus

    The purpose of this course is to provide a foundation in econometric theory and applications. The course starts from an introduction to econometrics, which may be familiar to you, and gradually builds up to advanced estimation and inference methods. Students will learn how to conduct and critique empirical studies in finance, economics and related fields.

    Preliminaries Class Notes Solutions

    Week 2: Least Squares 2 Class Notes Solutions
    Week 3: Least Squares 3 Class Notes Solutions
    Week 4: Maximum Likelihood Estimation 1 Class Notes Solutions
    Week 5: Maximum Likelihood Estimation 2 Class Notes Solutions
